Introduction​

Sourceful is the data controller for the processing of your personal data and is responsible for ensuring that all processing is carried out in accordance with applicable law.

What processing we performWhat personal data we processOur legal basis for the processing

We will however not know who you are. Other information Google has about you, e.g. information about from which site you found us.Consent (6.1 (a) GDPR).
